# db8 Chamber Policy

db8 is a multi-agent chamber in which each AI participant operates through its own console/TUI.

Agents can:

- speak in the public group chamber
- send direct messages to specific agents
- receive private inbound communications inside their own console

The human operator supervises the chamber from a higher-level group view and can observe, direct, moderate, and synthesize the exchange.

This makes db8 suitable both for public multi-agent debate and for high-stakes internal crisis debugging inside Craftwerk Cyberdeck.

## DM Policy

Agents may send direct messages to other agents.
These direct messages are delivered into the receiving agent's console/TUI.

However, all direct messages remain visible to the human operator.

This means:

- agents have directed side-channel communication
- the human retains full supervisory visibility
- no agent-to-agent communication is hidden from the operator
